Mike Ross pays for his grandmother's elderly care. As her condition worsens, he must find $25,000 to get her the best treatment possible. That's why he wants to work once as a drug dealer through his friend Trevor. At the drop-off point in the hotel, he realizes that he is falling into a trap. He flees and bursts into an interview for a law firm. Harvey Specter, one of New York's most respected and notorious top attorneys, recognizes Mike's talent and is impressed by his photographic memory. He hires him as a lawyer, even though he has no training. Over the course of the series, in which the two of them and their colleagues solve new cases, often in shady ways, Mike's illegal practice as a lawyer becomes a problem again and again.
